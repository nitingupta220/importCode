Drama
1. Either
(a) In plays, no one arrives on or leaves from the stage without contributing in some way to the
complexity of the play. Considering two or three plays you have studied, compare the impact
on meaning of some arrivals and departures from the stage.
Or
(b) What dramatic techniques have playwrights used to convey ideas and/or beliefs in two or three
plays you have studied, and how effective have they been?
Poetry

world to themselves. Considering in detail how poets in your study convey their principal
concerns, say to what extent you agree with the above statement. You must refer closely to the
work of two or three poets in your study and base your answer on a total of three or four poems.
